About Me

4+ years of Shopify expertise, one mission: helping brands scale to 7-figures.

My Journey

I started my Shopify journey in 2022 when the platform was still a relatively new concept. While others were focused on other platforms, I saw the future: brand-owned stores, data-driven advertising, and premium customer experiences.

Over the past 4+ years, I've managed over $500k in ad spend, optimized hundreds of stores, and helped brands grow from struggling startups to 8-figure businesses. But more importantly, I've learned that there's no substitute for hands-on expertise.

"Agencies will promise you the world. I promise you transparency, strategy, and results you can measure."

What I Believe

The Shopify landscape has changed dramatically. What worked 2 years ago doesn't work today. I'm constantly testing, learning, and adapting my strategies to stay ahead of the curve.

  • Data over intuition — Every decision is backed by metrics
  • Long-term thinking — Sustainable growth beats short-term wins
  • Transparency — You'll always know exactly what's happening with your store
  • Education — My goal is to make you self-sufficient, not dependent on me

What I Bring to the Table

Unlike agencies that rotate account managers every 6 months, you work directly with me. I've seen every scenario imaginable—from launches that tank to brands that hit bestseller status within weeks.

I don't use cookie-cutter approaches. Every brand gets a custom strategy based on their unique market position, competition, and growth goals.
